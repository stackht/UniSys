Complete ERP Coverage for College Administration

Comprehensive modules for administrators, registrar, COE, finance, HR, facilities, compliance, and student services.

Admissions & Registrar

  • Lead capture, counseling, applications, merit lists, seat allocation
  • Student Information System (SIS), ID generation, profile lifecycle
  • Academic calendar, course registration, add/drop, credit transfer
  • Document verification, eligibility checks, migration certificates

Attendance (Geofenced)

  • Geofenced check-in, timetable mapped attendance
  • Proxy detection, device validation, late/early thresholds
  • Parent/student alerts, shortage lists, remedial flags
  • Audit trails for every attendance event

Exams & Results

  • Exam schedules, hall tickets, invigilator allocation
  • Mark entry, moderation, revaluation, transcripts
  • Outcome/attainment mapping, CO/PO reports
  • Result publishing, grade sheets, certificates

Finance & Fees

  • Fee structures, scholarships, concessions, fines
  • Invoicing, payment gateways, reconciliation
  • Budgeting, cost centers, fund tracking
  • Audit-ready financial reports

HR & Payroll

  • Recruitment, onboarding, workload, appraisal
  • Payroll, benefits, tax, leave, attendance sync
  • Faculty CAS workflows, promotions, training
  • Staff roles, access control, separation of duties

Library & Resources

  • Cataloging, circulation, fines, e-resources
  • Interlibrary loans, usage analytics
  • Research repository, plagiarism integration
  • Asset tagging and procurement tracking

Hostel & Transport

  • Room allocation, occupancy, visitor logs
  • Mess plans, attendance, safety checks
  • Transport routes, GPS tracking, passes
  • Maintenance schedules and incident logs

Placements & Alumni

  • Company pipeline, drives, offers, internships
  • Alumni engagement, mentorship, fundraising
  • Career readiness tracking and analytics
  • Placement compliance reports

Facilities & Inventory

  • Asset inventory, AMC schedules, tickets
  • Energy usage, space utilization, audits
  • Procurement workflows, vendor management
  • Stores, stock levels, issue/return

Compliance & Accreditation

  • NAAC/NBA/AICTE/UGC evidence mapping
  • SSR/SAR/AQAR documentation workflows
  • Audit trails, policy versioning, approvals
  • Ranking and regulatory reporting packs

Governance & Communication

  • Role-based access, approvals, escalations
  • Announcements, SMS/email/app notifications
  • Service desk, SLAs, grievance redressal
  • Integration hub (LMS, biometrics, payment)
